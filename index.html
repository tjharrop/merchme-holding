EVERY BAR, CAFE, PUB AND RESTAURANT IS EMPTY! Buy some rad stuff instead?

The global COVID-19 outbreak is really shit for everyone. The bars, cafes, pubs, and restaurants who usually dish out love are putting the brakes on and doing their bit to stem the COVID-19 spread, even though it hurts their business.

Hospothreads.com is a place for hospitality businesses to connect with people who need stuff during the down-time.

It lets us, the people, show love to the legends who are risking their jobs and businesses to do their bit for society. There's up-to-date coverage on delivery and collections services from restaurants, and info about where you can buy merch, vouchers, and experiences from your favourite indie hospo crowd.
